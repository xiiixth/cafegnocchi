About

The Owner

Cafe Gnocchi was founded by Xeth Watin, a native Italian with a lifelong connection to the world of coffee. Raised in the heart of Italy's coffee region, Xeth has imbibed the art of crafting the perfect espresso from generations of family tradition.

Co-Owner

Xeth and Vincent are the driving force behind the vision of Cafe Gnocchi. Each with a distinct background, their paths converged around a mutual love for Italian coffee. Whether it's Xeth's deep roots in Italian traditions or Vincent's expertise in modern coffee-making techniques, their synergy forms the foundation of our café.
